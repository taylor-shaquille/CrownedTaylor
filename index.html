Welcome to our Wedding Website

We’ve created this website as a convenient and interactive way to share all of the important details with you in the lead up to our wedding. You can read more about our love story, get to know our awesome bridal party, and RSVP. Thank you for your ongoing love and support. We are so excited to share this day with you and look forward to celebrating Life and Love with all of our favorite people!




When we met in January 2017 we never would have imagined we would be where we are today. Me from New Orleans and him from Mobile. Me in the Navy and him in the Army. Finding each other at the Post Office was nothing short of a blessing. Shaquille's perspective: Michael was watching me for awhile until he got up the courage to ask for my number. Our relationship journey went from not talking for 2 weeks to driving 40 minutes everyday to see each other. He proposed privately in our apartment one night after dinner. His hands were shaking and I could tell he was nervous. He didn't have to be because I couldn't wait to say yes and spend the rest of my life with him. Michael's perspective: We locked eyes and Shaquille gave me a look that let me know she wanted me to walk up to her and ask for her number. Then one night I was at the movies alone and receieved a text from her that would begin to plant the seed for our future. We became inseparable. A week before my birthday I proposed to her and got the emotional response I wanted. Now I can't wait until the day I call her my wife. Fate has brought us to this very moment.
